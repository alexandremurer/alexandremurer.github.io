Renewing Rouen Area - France

Urban Planning Contest Proposal

As many industrially-grown cities, Rouen suffers from aging infrastructures which future is unsure. In order to keep the city developing on its economic and build heritage, our team proposed a comprehensive, sustainability-focused plan to guide local renewal.

Our proposals were based on the notion of “landscape cultivation”. Through this idea gathering the ecologic elements (with natural landscape) and the industrial past of the area (economic landscape), I worked on the programming of several urban activators to enhance the leverage of existing and future projects.

  • Date: Spring 2017
  • Client: Ergapolis, Rouen Métropole, Ville de Rouen, AURBSE
  • Category: Urban Design, Urban Programming, GIS, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Oral Speeches

Programming Compiegne - France

Scholar Group Project

Compiègne is a rapidly-expanding city located in the North of France. The local university attracts new businesses and the city is looking to house its new workers. In plus of this incoming population, the city registers increasing divorces for its domestic households. The vision for the development of Compiègne is thus balanced between the situations of its housing needs.

Based on a comprehensive diagnosis, our team proposed housing units adapted to the diversity of uses needed to welcome new households. But the site of development was also challenging since located on an urban fringe close to the local forest. To bring economic development, social housing and sustainability constraints, we proposed an urban program focused on the transition urban-forest

  • Date: Spring 2018
  • Client: -
  • Category: Urban Design, Urban Programming, GIS, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

Developing Lille - France

Professional Project

Lille Area is the sixth largest urbanized region of France. Located between Paris, London and Brussels, the city enjoys an ongoing economic development. Lesquin is the city on which Lille’s airport is located. To attract mobile businesses close to Lille, the local economic authority has a plan to build a 100-acres business park in Lesquin.

As an intern at Ville Ouverte for this project, I had the opportunity to do a lot of research, benchmarks, and short studies to understand how business parks are structured in European airports’ areas. I also worked with my colleagues to identify the kind of services to implement on site while matching J. Rifkins’ 3rd revolution ideas. After 4 months of work with urban-architects, landscape designers and experts in economy, we were able to propose a masterplan for the business park.

  • Date: Spring 2017
  • Client: CCI Grand Lille, Métropole Européenne de Lille, Ville de Lesquin
  • Category: Urban Programming, Interviews, Stakeholders Engagement, Adobe Illustrator

Designing Paris’s Iconic Public Space - France

Scholar Group Project

The Southern University of Paris is an institution for research in sciences: the university has four Fields Medal and two Nobel Prize winners. Despite its international fame, the so-called Université Paris-Sud suffers from a leak of students and researchers joining the Paris-Saclay cluster for research and entrepreneurship, located a few kilometers away from the prestigious campus.

As a Ville Ouverte intern, I intervened during the diagnosis phase of this project, for which I conducted a couple of interview with scholar stakeholders and students. Through a deep understanding of the university, local public organization and students’ needs for future uses of the campus, I helped identifying the lacks and surplus of services on campus.

  • Date: Spring 2017
  • Client: -
  • Category: Urban Design, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Placemaking
